About Me

I am a Postdoctoral associate at the Virtual Experiences Research Group at the University of Florida. My research interests are in multimodal interaction combining tangible user interfaces with multi-touch, augmented, and virtual reality. I am also interested in the maker movement, and how rapid digital prototyping can be brought to the masses. I have been spending most of my time lately exploring and implementing Virtual 3D Social Spaces for enhanced learning environments. I have collaborated with multi-disciplinary teams of musicians, communication scientists, and physicians both from the academy and the industry across multiple projects. I enjoy designing, making things, and tinkering with them.
