UNIVERSITY OF FLORIDA | Post Doctoral Associate

I’ve worked in the Virtual Experiences Research Group (VERG) since Fall 2019. During my time in VERG, I’ve collaborated with several interdisciplinary teams in projects involving virtual humans for mental health communication and skills training. I have also developed several virtual 3D social spaces for enhanced learning and presented that work in a number of workshops, guest-lectures, and presentations to faculty and instructors from all over the state.  

CLEMSON UNIVERSITY | Graduate Research Assistant

I worked in the Tangible Visualization Research Group (TangViz) as I pursued my PhD degree from Fall 2014 to Fall 2019 under Dr. Brygg Ullmer. During this time, I researched the intersection of tangible and multi-touch interaction and virtual reality. I was TA for several classes including Interface Design and Technology (IDT) and Operating Systems, and I worked and mentored a number of brilliant undergrad and high-school students.

ACCENTURE BRAZIL | SAP Project Manager

After graduating from the University of São Paulo as a Bachelor in Computer Science, I joined Accenture Brazil. There, I worked in several Data Warehousing and SAP related projects as a developer, team lead, and project manager. When I left Accenture to pursue my PhD, I was a Loss Prevention specialist at the Center of Excellence in utilities working for power, water, and gas companies in the state of São Paulo.
